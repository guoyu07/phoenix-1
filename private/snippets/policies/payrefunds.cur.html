Tuition Payment

Your enrollments are not guaranteed until you make full payment, including any fees associated with each enrollment transaction. Please review our payment policy below.

General Items

  • Late payment is not accepted and will be subject to a late fee of 10%. CIS reserves the right to release your child's place in any course for which payment has not been received by the due date.
  • Late enrollment is subject to availability at the time that we receive full payment. It is suggested that you make late registrations in person.
  • Receipts are only given if requested after an in-person payment. We do not mail out receipts. Latest payment information will always be available online.
  • Please make crossed cheques payable to Chinese International School.
  • In the event of a bounced cheque, we reserve the right to charge an additional bank processing fee. You will be contacted if such a fee will be levied.

Payment Methods

Payments are accepting using the methods described below only. At this time, CIS does not accept credit card, traveler's cheques or money order of any kind.

  • Self-reporting cheque payment: This is our new, recommended payment method. You may at your convenience, before payment due date, login to our website to make a payment by entering cheque details (account number, cheque amount) and mailing a cheque. Our office must receive your mailed physical cheque with matching details within 5 business days, or the payment will be marked as invalid.
  • In-person cheque payment: You may hand in your cheque to our office directly at any time the office is open. You may request a receipt using this payment method. Please hand in your cheque before the payment deadline.
  • Electronic funds transfer: We do not offer this payment method online or in person and is meant as a convenience for families outside Hong Kong who do not have established Hong Kong banking accounts. You must receive authorisation from the Summer Program and CIS Business Office prior to making a payment. Fees may apply.
  • Cash: Only available for change fee or late add fee payment and must be paid in cash. Do not mail cash. A receipt will be issued upon request.

Refund Policy

Please review our terms and conditions for refunds of any kind in conjunction with the Summer Program.

  • Refunds will be given on the tuition paid minus any miscellaneous charges including, but not limited to: late/change admin fees, course materials fees, and reimbursement fees.
  • Refund and withdrawal requests must be made within the deadline (see Fee & Tuition Schedule above) to receive a 100% refund.
  • A returned cheque (in person only) shall suffice as a full refund. You may also request that we shred your cheque.
  • Refunds on alternative payments must be pre-authorised by CIS personnel and could take several weeks to process.
  • Withdrawal requests will be charged a 25% administration charge if made after the deadline for each separate week withdrawn. This charge will be taken out of your total refund for that week.
