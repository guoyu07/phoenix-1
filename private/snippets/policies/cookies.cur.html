Cookie Policy

Some countries around the world today have enacted laws to protect their citizens' privacy, especially through the use of website cookies. Cookies are small text-based files that store information commonly used to track users' data as they traverse through the Internet.

The CIS Summer Programme website uses cookies for two reasons: (a) to authenticate users so we can provide them with interactive, customised information such as child information and course enrolmentx data, and (b) to report metrics on the computer and network environment of each visitor for internal use and improvements to the site.

You may opt to remove our cookies for one session at the bottom of this page. Please note that this feature requires JavaScript.
