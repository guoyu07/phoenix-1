Methods of Payment

CIS accepts tuition payment by cheque only. Your family account will have a running bill of all credited items and payments. If you will be mailing cheques, we recommend pre-confirm your payment online so we can process your payment faster. We must receive either the cheque itself or this pre-confirmation before the due date or the system will drop enrolment on all late items.

Please make your cheques payable to Chinese International School. Receipts are given only upon request and only at the Programme office.

Non-Hong Kong Residents

For students coming to Hong Kong to attend the Summer Programme, you may arrange an Electronic Funds Transfer as payment in certain circumstances. An additional fee may apply. Please make a Help Desk request for alternative payment after logging in to your account. We will reply with the details necessary for you to make this transfer.

Transfers must be arranged as soon as possible in order to avoid an automatic cancellation of your enrolment due to late/unpai items.
