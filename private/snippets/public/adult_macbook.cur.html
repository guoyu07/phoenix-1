Adult Macbook Course

What is it?

At CIS, we understand that many parents are still getting accustomed to their children's seemingly heroic Mac and iDevice manipulation skills. This year, we're offering CIS parents and guardians exclusive access to an Beginner Adult Summer Course for Macbook usage, taught by our own Anita Chen!

Parents are required to bring their own Macbooks to the course as school computers will not be provided.

In this course, you will learn to master your Macbook so you can support your child’s learning at home. Learn more about iTunes, iPhoto and iMovie and support home learning. Anita Chen is the ICT Facilitator (Primary) at CIS. She is an Apple Distinguished Educator (ADE).
