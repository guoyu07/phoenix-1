The CIS Summer Programme

Welcome!

The CIS Summer Programme is one of Hong Kong's oldest continuously running international school summer school programmes, operating since the summer of 1995. We're pleased to bring to you The CIS Summer Programme 2013!

This year's programme will run for four weeks. Like the 2012 Programme, we will be offering two different sessions of the programme catering to different needs: the traditional Summer Programme for kids age 5 and up, and the Academic Programme for students who have just graduated Years 9 through 12 (that's Form 3/8th Grade through Form 6/11th Grade).

The Summer School

The mind is not a vessel to be filled, but a fire to be kindled. Plutarch, Roman author and historian

Our Summer School for children ages 5 and up has been running continuously since 1995 and we aim to provide the safest, most enjoyable learning experience during the summer. Many of our students come from outside CIS as well, with parent and child alike coming to see what CIS has to offer.

This year, our courses will be split into five categories:

  • The Arts ARTS: Offering both visual and performing arts courses, such as Chinese calligraphy, pottery, cartoon drawing, hip-hop dance, filmmaking. These courses are favourites for many of our children.
  • Languages LANG: Let your child's creativity and linguistic power flourish wish our English and Chinese classes in varying degrees of difficulty.
  • Mathematics and Critical Thinking MACT: Our maths courses introduce and review concepts learned in day school, from simple arithmetic up to middle and high-school level mathematics. Plus, let your child learn chess or Go in our new critical thinking category of classes!
  • Science, Computers and Technology SCIT: A plethora of science exploration and investigation courses, and new this year - computer courses ranging from learning how to type to making websites (almost as beautiful as this one!)
  • Physical Education PHED: Exercise is important - and there's nowhere better to do it than at a school that sports (no pun intended) an indoor swimming pool, tennis and basketball courts and several indoor gymnasiums.

Make sure you preview our course list before you even register! You'll get to see these courses as early as March as we release them.

The Academic Programme

Give a man a fish and you feed him for a day. Teach a man to fish and you feed him for a lifetime. Confucius, Chinese philosopher

The Senior Academic Programme was first introduced in the 2012 Programme as an afternoon programme to help students in secondary school with IB and SAT exam preparation. This year, we're proud to expand that programme into the morning, and offer a larger selection of courses.

IB Courses

All our IB courses are taught by CIS teachers who have had many years of experience in teaching their subject. CIS is one of the first schools in Hong Kong to offer IB (both the Diploma Program and the Middle Years Program) and has consistently produced graduating cohorts with some of the best results in the world. We invite you and your child to take part in this valuabl experience!

  • IB Taster Courses IBTC: Restricted to incoming Years 10-12, these broad courses give students a general idea of what to expect in certain IB subjects, such as Economics or Biology. It also helps students get prepared for their entrance into IB studies.
  • IB Review IBRV: These courses, for incoming Year 13, will review the material taught in the past year and prepare students for their last year of secondary school.

University Applications Writing Courses

(Code UNIW) This year, we are also offering students the chance to draft, review, and edit their university application written statements with CIS teachers and staff. Writing university app statements can be a difficult and arduous task, but these courses will help your child plan, write and improve every personal statment or application essay that they write.

Our Campus & Facilities

All classes in the both programmes are held within the beautiful CIS campus and taught by current and former CIS teachers. Our day school is one of the most prestigious and exclusive in the city. We guarantee your child will love not only the classes, but also our accredited learning environment, friendly teachers and staff and of course, fellow energetic students.

Over the years, CIS has built a full-featured campus, comprising of everything you'd expect a school to have, and more. Some of our facilities include:

  • two libraries
  • four gymnasiums
  • a 25-meter indoor heated swimming pool
  • fitniss room
  • outdoor climbing wall
  • 200-seat auditorium
  • 200-seat drama studio
  • science labs
  • fully wifi-equipped

CIS is located on top of Braemar Hill, North Point, and is well served by busses and minibuses with connections to Causeway Bay, Fortress Hill and North Point MTR stations.
