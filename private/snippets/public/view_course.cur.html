Course Notes

Parents please take note of these following items regarding this course.

Computer usage: Students in this course will use school computers extensively for the duration of the class.

Musical instrument usage: This class will require students to use school musical instruments, such as guitars or keyboards.

Additional fees: An addition fee may apply to students registering in this class.

Physical activity: Students will be required to perform physical activity in this course, and are recommended to wear comfortable, loose-fitting, sporty clothing.

Age enforcement: Students who do not meet the age range for this course cannot register due to safety or other operational reasons. PTE's are disabled for this course as a result.

Waitlist available: In the event that this course fills up, you will be allowed to select this course on a waitlist basis and may be enrolled if another student drops the course.
